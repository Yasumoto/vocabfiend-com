VocabFiend

Create amazing stories with your friends!

The core of VocabFiend is telling stories with your friends. With a simple, clean interface, you are empowered to let your imagination run wild while using new words to direct the story!

If you have a friend who is studying for an upcoming test, you can help them study by telling stories which use difficult and new words!

Improve your vocabulary for the GRE!

By removing the typical time limits, points values, and other judgements from typical games, we have found that VocabFiend's formula reduces stress and lets students focus on the words they want to improve upon. The current core list is aimed at the GRE standardized test.
